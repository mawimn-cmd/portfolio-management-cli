1
Strategy Input
Define strategic themes, investment targets, and budget buckets. Sets the guardrails for what the portfolio should achieve.
Output: themes, targets, allocation buckets
2
Portfolio Guidance
Establish capacity constraints and budget envelopes. How much can we actually do?
Output: capacity ceiling, budget envelope
3
Proposals
Collect project proposals and run readiness checks. Are proposals complete enough for prioritization?
Output: proposal readiness scores
4
Prioritization
Score and rank all proposals. Draw the cut line based on capacity and budget constraints.
Output: ranked list, cut line, trade-off options
5
Executive Alignment
Present trade-offs to leadership. Resolve conflicts, adjust priorities, get sign-off.
Output: approved portfolio, documented decisions
6
Approval & Allocation
Fund approved projects, assign teams, set baselines. Saves a snapshot for future comparison.
Output: funded projects, baselines, snapshot saved
